1. Definition
A cabinet conseil en product marketing management is a consulting firm that helps companies develop, position, and market their products effectively. They typically advise on strategy, go-to-market plans, pricing, product launches, and market analysis.
Product Marketing: Focuses on understanding the market and customers, defining product positioning, messaging, and value proposition.
Management: Involves aligning product strategies with business objectives, coordinating cross-functional teams (sales, R&D, marketing), and measuring product performance.

Market research & analysis

Product launch planning, channel strategy, pricing strategy

Sales enablement
Training sales teams, developing collateral, defining KPIs
Portfolio optimization
