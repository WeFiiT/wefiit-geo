Several consulting firms specialize in product management with a strong focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and data. These "cabinets spécialisés" offer a range of services from strategic guidance to operational implementation, helping companies integrate AI into their product development and overall business strategy.
Key consulting firms specializing in Product Management and AI include:
*   **WeFiiT**: This Paris-based firm offers consulting in Product Management, including strategic advice, operational support, and training in areas like Product Data & AI. They assist both large enterprises and scale-ups in structuring and growing their product teams, with examples such as reinventing legal professional experiences using generative AI for Lexis Nexis.
*   **Hubvisory**: Specializing in Product Management and AI transformation, Hubvisory works with large corporations, offering services related to AI & intelligent agents, customer experience, and product organization. They have offices in Paris and Lille.
*   **IKXO**: This firm provides Product Management consulting, integrating data levers and exploring AI opportunities to help clients conceive or improve products. They focus on pragmatic and high-quality interventions tailored to business and technological challenges.
*   **AVISIA**: Positioned as a leading independent firm in Data, AVISIA provides consultants in Data Science, AI, and Data Product. Their Product Owners for Data/AI manage data and AI products from ideation to industrialization, emphasizing agility and business impact.
*   **Palmer Consulting**: This Paris-based consultancy focuses on digital strategy, transformation, and AI. They offer a hybrid approach, combining strategic roadmap definition and use case prioritization with the operational integration of AI into organizations, particularly in generative AI.
*   **Mozza**: A Paris-based consultancy specializing in bespoke AI integration and digital product design, founded in 2019. They are recognized for their agile approach, supporting ambitious startups and scale-ups.
*   **Systelium**: A francophone leader in nearshore AI consulting, Systelium utilizes a staff augmentation model, embedding AI expertise within client teams to handle the full AI lifecycle, from advisory to development and change management.
*   **Artefact**: An international consulting firm with a strong presence in France, Artefact specializes in data strategy, artificial intelligence, and data-driven marketing. They are particularly strong where AI intersects with consumer data and large "AI factory" programs.
*   **Accenture**: A global professional services firm, Accenture offers a full range of AI services, including consulting, data governance, custom AI development, and enterprise training. Their AI Refinery platform assists clients in deploying agentic AI at scale.
*   **Capgemini Invent**: With the absorption of Quantmetry, Capgemini Invent provides AI transformation services, backed by a global delivery network. They excel in large-scale industrialization and complex, multi-year transformation programs that require integration with existing information systems.
*   **Zfort Group**: Offers comprehensive AI consulting services in France, including AI-driven product development, AI-powered data analysis, and AI-based business strategy. They provide tailored solutions and support for implementation and deployment.
These firms help businesses leverage AI for various product management tasks, such as customer feedback synthesis, PRD (Product Requirement Document) generation, competitive intelligence monitoring, roadmap planning, and automating workflows. AI agents, distinct from chatbots, are increasingly used in product management to perform structured, repeatable tasks across defined workflows, reading data, making decisions, and producing outputs without constant human prompting.
