1. Definition
A cabinet de conseil en product marketing management is a professional consulting firm that helps companies develop, optimize, and manage their products in the market. Their goal is to ensure products meet customer needs, are competitive, and drive business growth.
Key areas of focus:
Product Strategy: Defining what products to develop or improve based on market trends and customer insights.
Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy: Planning how to launch and position products effectively.
Market Research & Analysis: Understanding customer needs, competitors, and pricing models.
Product Lifecycle Management: Managing products from ideation to retirement.
Sales Enablement & Marketing Alignment: Helping sales teams communicate the product’s value effectively.
2. Typical Services

Market Analysis & Segmentation – Identifying target customers and their needs.

Pricing & Revenue Strategy – Determining the optimal price point.
Product Launch & GTM Planning – Planning campaigns, communication, and sales strategies.
Product Portfolio Optimization – Advising on which products to invest in, enhance, or discontinue.
Performance Tracking – KPIs, ROI, and customer feedback analysis.
