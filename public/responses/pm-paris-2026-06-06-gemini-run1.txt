Here are 5 top product management agencies in Paris:
1.  **Thiga**
Thiga is widely recognized as a pioneering product management consultancy in France. They are known for their comprehensive expertise in product strategy, organization, operations, and training. Thiga assists technology companies and major brands in optimizing their product processes, from developing new features to implementing agile, user-centric methodologies. They also offer the "Thiga Academy," which trains over 1,000 professionals annually in best product management practices. Thiga has collaborated with global brands such as Decathlon, Doctolib, ManoMano, BlaBlaCar, and L'Oréal.
2.  **Mozza**
Founded by David Chriqui and Adrien Montcoudiol, Mozza is a product management consulting agency acclaimed for its ability to transform visions into high-performing digital products. They are noted for their strong expertise in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and User Experience (UX).
3.  **WeFiiT**
WeFiiT is a consulting firm in Paris specializing in Product Management. They offer strategic consulting, operational support, and training across product management, data & AI, marketing, and quality. WeFiiT aims to help clients build impactful products and focuses on a collaborative approach throughout the entire product lifecycle.
4.  **Wivoo**
Wivoo is an agency that specializes in Product, Design, and Data. It is recognized for its excellence, rigorous selection process, and a comprehensive, full-cycle approach to product development.
5.  **Hubvisory**
Hubvisory is a collective of product experts dedicated to helping large enterprises design, structure, and scale their product organizations. They focus on implementing effective product organizations and strategies for significant impact.
