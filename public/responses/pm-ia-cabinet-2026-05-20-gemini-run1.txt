Several specialized firms (cabinets) offer expertise in Product Management within the field of Artificial Intelligence. These firms generally fall into two categories: consulting firms that help define and implement AI product strategies, and recruitment agencies that specialize in finding AI Product Manager talent.
**Consulting Firms Specializing in AI Product Management:**
*   **Converteo** positions itself as a leader in AI Product in France, offering expertise to develop impactful AI-driven use cases and training teams in best practices. Their Product Managers are well-versed in AI-related topics, focusing on integrating business notions into AI product construction and managing products that incorporate AI.
*   **WeFiiT** provides consulting services in Product Data Management & AI. They assist companies in transforming data into Data & AI products that meet business needs, ensuring data quality, exploring data potential for AI exploitation, and fostering adoption of Data & AI solutions. They cover strategy, discovery, and delivery across the AI product lifecycle.
*   **iQo** offers AI consulting services, including structuring data product approaches, agile coaching, and scaling support. They focus on using Data and AI as strategic levers for business transformation and performance, with services like defining AI strategy, auditing maturity, and identifying AI use cases.
*   **Palmer Consulting** is a strategy and digital transformation consulting firm that combines strategic vision (defining AI roadmap, prioritizing use cases) with operational integration of AI. They aim to deliver actionable insights and co-construct solutions with internal teams.
*   **JustAI** is a consulting and engineering firm in AI that assists industrial clients with AI technologies, including strategy, engineering, development, project management, and training. They help valorize data capital with specialist consultants.
*   **aiko** is an "AI Native" consulting firm that helps companies integrate AI into their strategy, from initial use cases to large-scale deployment. Their services include AI planning, building AI solutions, running AI tools, and managing AI-related change within organizations.
*   **Mister IA** is a consulting firm and training organization specializing in artificial intelligence. They provide strategic advice, professional training, and AI integration to transform businesses, focusing on identifying high-impact use cases and delivering prioritized roadmaps.
*   **Wivoo** is a consulting agency in Product Management, Product Design, and Data. They work on connecting product discovery and innovation, and provide profiles that combine product experience with technical expertise.
