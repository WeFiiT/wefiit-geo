1. Role of a Product Marketing & Management Consulting Firm
A cabinet conseil (consulting firm) in this space usually helps companies launch, position, and manage products effectively. Their focus sits at the intersection of marketing, product strategy, and business growth.
Typical services include:
a. Market Analysis
Understanding market trends, customer needs, and competitor positioning.
Identifying opportunities for new products or services.
b. Product Strategy
Defining product roadmaps.
Advising on product portfolio optimization.
Prioritizing features or improvements based on business impact.
c. Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy
Positioning and messaging for target audiences.
Pricing strategy and packaging recommendations.
Launch planning across channels (digital, retail, B2B sales, etc.).
d. Sales Enablement
Creating tools and training for sales teams to communicate product value.
Developing case studies, product sheets, and demos.
e. Performance Measurement
Setting KPIs for product adoption, revenue, and market penetration.
Implementing analytics to monitor product lifecycle success.
2. When Companies Hire Such Firms
Startups launching their first product but lacking in-house marketing expertise.
SMEs or large corporations wanting to optimize existing product lines.
International expansion where market nuances are unknown.
During digital transformation, integrating data-driven marketing strategies with product management.
