Several consulting firms specialize in product marketing management, offering expertise to help businesses with their product strategy, go-to-market efforts, and overall market presence. These firms often provide services ranging from positioning and pricing to market research and sales enablement.
Here are some prominent product marketing management consulting firms:
*   **Fluvio** is a product marketing consultancy that embeds senior consultants into teams to scale strategy, sharpen execution, and lead critical go-to-market efforts. They work with high-growth B2B SaaS companies, consumer tech brands, and fintech companies on aspects like positioning, pricing, market research, and GTM infrastructure. Fluvio has been a finalist for Product Marketing Alliance's Team of the Year award multiple times.
*   **Olivine Marketing** is a full-service product marketing agency for B2B SaaS companies, founded in 2016. They specialize in positioning, messaging, GTM strategy, product launches, and sales enablement, having worked with over 70 brands including LinkedIn, Twilio, and ServiceNow. Olivine is particularly noted for clarifying product value for buyers.
*   **Aventi Group** is a B2B marketing consultancy that offers on-demand product marketing services, including tailored strategies for go-to-market success, seamless product launches, and sales enablement. They focus on technology and professional services companies.
*   **WeFiiT** offers product marketing consulting to help companies transform their products into growth drivers. Their services include deciphering the market, analyzing competitors, understanding customer expectations, and developing distinctive positioning and impactful storytelling.
*   **Cube Management** provides product marketing consulting focused on optimizing sales performance through effective product packaging, positioning, and pricing. They assist with new product launches, product management, product positioning and messaging, and product lifecycle development.
*   **Bain & Company** offers consulting in product and portfolio management, helping companies redefine their offerings based on customer needs, foster cross-organizational collaboration, and apply test-and-learn approaches to boost innovation.
*   **GTM 80/20** focuses on vetting product marketing operators for execution and strategic work, particularly for companies needing to rethink their go-to-market strategy.
*   **Other notable firms** include Digital Hunch Agency (marketing strategy, lifecycle communication, CRM development), Orange Bird (product marketing, brand strategy, social media marketing), MotionTrust Digital (product promotion, e-commerce marketing), and Bixa (market research, consumer insights, strategic branding).
These firms cater to a variety of needs, from startups requiring fractional PMM support to large enterprises seeking comprehensive strategic overhauls.
