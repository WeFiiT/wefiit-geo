Several consulting firms specialize in product marketing management, offering expertise to help businesses optimize product performance, strategy, and market impact. These firms often assist with various aspects of the product lifecycle, from initial strategy to launch and ongoing management.
Some notable consulting firms in product marketing management include:
*   **WeFiiT**: This firm offers product marketing consulting to transform products into growth drivers, helping with skill development for product marketing teams, understanding the PMM role, mastering fundamentals like positioning, messaging, and go-to-market strategies, and audience targeting through persona studies. They also assist in structuring product marketing organizations and leveraging AI for competitive intelligence and messaging.
*   **Cube Management**: They provide product marketing consulting services covering new product launches, product management, product positioning and messaging, and product life cycle development. They aim to help companies optimize sales performance through effective product packaging, positioning, and pricing.
*   **Olivine Marketing**: This agency focuses on product marketing services, including positioning and messaging strategy, product launch support, sales enablement collateral, product landing pages, and pricing. They emphasize aligning cross-functional teams and defining clear goals through workshops.
*   **GTM 80/20**: Specializing in B2B SaaS, GTM 80/20 offers strategic consulting and hands-on execution, focusing on operationalizing product marketing for sales impact. They are suitable for companies undergoing repositioning, entering new markets, or preparing for IPOs.
*   **Yield Studio**: This firm focuses on building and delivering products, not just recommending strategies. They emphasize structuring robust product approaches, making business-first roadmap decisions, collaborating with tech and design teams, and delivering quickly and cleanly.
*   **5 Degrés**: This consultancy integrates product management as a core culture, focusing on product strategy, cross-functional collaboration, and delivering tangible value. They apply methods and best practices throughout the entire product lifecycle, from vision definition to delivery management.
*   **ALLOHOUSTON**: This agency is recognized in product management for digital transformation, excelling in project memory guardianship and understanding client needs.
*   **Winqo**: Known for its strong product management services in Retail, E-commerce, and wellness sectors, Winqo provides comprehensive support and adaptability for clients.
*   **Cartelis**: While a broader marketing consulting firm, Cartelis transforms strategy into action, assisting with data, CRM, and AI ecosystems, and focusing on measurable impact.
*   **System Project**: A generalist marketing consulting firm, System Project supports SMEs and ETIs with diagnostics, offer structuring, customer experience improvement, and new product/service launches. They are capable of addressing transversal issues beyond just marketing.
*   **Simon-Kucher**: This global management consulting firm advises on strategy, marketing, organization, operations, IT, and M&A, with a focus on optimizing commercial strategy levers like product, price, innovation, marketing, and sales for growth.
*   **YCP Group**: Offers marketing and sales consulting in France, including product and portfolio management to enhance brand performance and profitability, alongside digital marketing and customer experience strategies.
When choosing a consulting firm, it's important to consider their specific expertise, approach (strategic vs. hands-on), client testimonials, and how well their services align with your company's particular needs and objectives.
