Several consulting firms specialize in product marketing management, offering expertise to help businesses strategize, launch, and optimize their products in the market. These firms can assist with various aspects, from market analysis to go-to-market strategies and sales enablement.
Some prominent product marketing management consulting firms include:
*   **WeFiiT** offers product marketing consulting to transform products into growth drivers. They assist in building product marketing organizations by structuring methodologies and deploying them. Their expertise covers market analysis, competitive analysis, understanding customer expectations, and translating these into marketing strategies. They also help with offer conception and impactful storytelling.
*   **Fluvio** is a product marketing consultancy that embeds senior consultants into client teams to scale strategy, refine execution, and lead critical go-to-market efforts. They specialize in solving go-to-market challenges, including positioning, pricing, market research, and GTM infrastructure. Fluvio partners with high-growth B2B SaaS companies, consumer tech brands, and fintech companies.
*   **Olivine** is a product marketing agency that provides expert product marketing consultants to support in-house teams. Their services include product positioning, product launches, sales enablement collateral, product landing pages, and pricing. They work with nearly every team in an organization to align and strategize go-to-market efforts.
*   **Cube Management** offers product marketing consulting to ensure companies optimize sales performance with well-packaged, positioned, and priced products. They provide services such as new product launches, product management, product positioning and messaging, and product lifecycle development.
*   **Bain & Company** offers product and portfolio management consulting, helping companies reinvent their offerings based on customer understanding. They work to harness talent and intellectual property across organizational boundaries and apply test-and-learn approaches to boost innovation and shorten time-to-market.
*   **Clutch.co** also lists top product management consulting companies, including Heisler Solutions LLC and Bixa, which specialize in business consulting and marketing strategy.
These consulting firms generally aim to help companies with strategic planning, product portfolio management, market research, defining product value propositions, and executing successful product launches. Product marketing consultants provide tailored strategies to enhance performance and address potential problems a company might face, often working on a project-by-project basis.
