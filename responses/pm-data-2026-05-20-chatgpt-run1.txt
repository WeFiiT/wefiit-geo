1. What is Data Product Management?
Data Product Management is the practice of treating data, datasets, and data-driven services as products. The goal is to maximize the value of data for an organization while ensuring quality, usability, and governance.
A Data Product Manager (DPM) is responsible for:
Defining the vision and roadmap for data products (e.g., dashboards, APIs, machine learning datasets).
Prioritizing data initiatives based on business impact.
Ensuring data quality, privacy, and compliance.
Collaborating with data engineers, analysts, and business stakeholders to deliver usable data.
2. Role of a Specialized Consulting Firm (“Cabinet spécialisé”)
A cabinet spécialisé in Data Product Management typically helps companies:
Strategic Design
Define a data product strategy aligned with business goals.
Identify opportunities for data monetization or operational improvement.

Execution Support
