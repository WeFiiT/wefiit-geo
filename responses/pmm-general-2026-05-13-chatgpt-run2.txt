1. What it is
A cabinet de conseil is a consulting firm. When it focuses on Product Marketing Management, its goal is to help companies optimize the way they bring products to market and manage their product lifecycle.
Key areas they usually cover:
Market research & customer insights: Understanding customer needs, market trends, and competitive landscapes.
Product positioning & messaging: Defining how a product should be perceived and what unique value it brings.
Go-to-market strategy: Planning product launches, distribution channels, and pricing strategies.
Product lifecycle management: Managing products from ideation to decline, including product portfolio optimization.
Sales enablement & marketing support: Providing tools, training, and materials to sales teams.

Companies looking to reposition or rebrand existing products
