Ah, you’re asking about cabinets spécialisés en Data Product Management — firms or consultancies that specifically focus on helping companies structure, manage, and scale their data products. Let’s break it down carefully.
1. What is a “Data Product Management” specialized firm?
A Data Product Manager (DPM) is responsible for turning data into valuable products or features. This is different from regular product management because:
The “product” is often data itself (datasets, analytics dashboards, APIs, ML models).
Success metrics are often data-driven, like adoption of a dataset, model accuracy, or impact on decision-making.
Requires cross-functional coordination: data engineers, data scientists, business stakeholders, legal/compliance teams.
A specialized cabinet in this area will typically offer services like:
Strategy & Governance
Define the data product portfolio.
Establish data product ownership and KPIs.
Implement data governance & quality standards.
Process & Frameworks
Set up Agile workflows for data products.
Define roadmaps for data services and ML pipelines.
Design data discovery & cataloging processes.
Product Design & Delivery
Build data APIs, dashboards, ML models as products.
Ensure user-centric design (data consumers in mind).
Monitor adoption and business impact.
Training & Upskilling
Train PMs, engineers, analysts on data product thinking.
Enable a data-driven culture across the company.
2. Examples of types of firms
Depending on the region (especially in Europe and North America), the following types of firms often specialize in this area:
Boutique Data Product Consultancies
Examples: Data Reply, Dataiku Consulting Services, some smaller specialized boutiques in Paris, London, or NYC.
Large Consulting Firms with Data Practice
Examples: McKinsey Analytics, BCG Gamma, Accenture Applied Intelligence, Capgemini Invent.
They have dedicated teams for data product strategy, though sometimes less “boutique” and more broad.
Data Platform/Tools Firms Offering Advisory
Snowflake, Databricks, Dataiku, ThoughtSpot sometimes offer consulting for implementing data products on their platforms.
