1. What is Data Product Management (DPM)?
Data Product Management is the discipline that focuses on treating data itself as a product. Instead of just collecting and storing data, companies:
Define data products (e.g., APIs, datasets, ML models, dashboards) with clear value for users.
Establish data governance and quality standards.
Use product management principles: roadmap, prioritization, KPIs, lifecycle management.
Ensure the data is usable, reliable, and monetizable if applicable.
A data product manager (DPM) is responsible for the lifecycle of data products, bridging business, analytics, and engineering teams.
