1. What is Data Product Management?
Data Product Management (DPM) is the discipline of managing data as a product. Unlike traditional product management, which focuses on features and user experience, DPM focuses on:
Data quality: ensuring data is accurate, consistent, and reliable.
Data accessibility: making it available to internal/external stakeholders.
Data governance & compliance: following privacy regulations like GDPR.
Data monetization: using data to generate business value.
Analytics and ML readiness: preparing data for AI/ML models.
A Data Product Manager (DPM) acts as the bridge between:
Technical teams (data engineers, analysts, data scientists)
Business teams (marketing, operations, finance)
