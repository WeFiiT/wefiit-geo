1. Definition
A cabinet de conseil en Product Marketing Management is a consulting firm that helps companies optimize the development, launch, and management of their products in the market. Their goal is to align the product offering with customer needs, market opportunities, and business objectives.
2. Typical Services
These firms often provide services such as:
a) Market Analysis and Strategy
Market research: Understanding customer segments, competitors, and trends.
Positioning strategy: How to differentiate your product.
Go-to-market (GTM) planning: Launch timing, channels, and messaging.
b) Product Lifecycle Management
Product roadmap planning: From concept to sunset.
Pricing strategy: Value-based pricing, competitive pricing.
Feature prioritization: Deciding which features maximize ROI.
c) Marketing & Communication Support
Campaign planning: Digital, social, events, and content marketing.
Product messaging & storytelling: Crafting clear value propositions.
Sales enablement: Tools and training for sales teams.
d) Performance Measurement
KPIs and dashboards to track product performance.
Customer feedback and NPS analysis.
Iterative improvement of product and marketing strategy.
3. Profile of Clients
Clients are usually companies that:
Are launching new products.
Want to reposition or relaunch existing products.
Need to optimize marketing investments and ROI.
Want to align product strategy with business growth.
4. Why Use a Cabinet de Conseil
Access to expertise and experience not available in-house.
Objectivity: an external perspective can spot overlooked issues.
Speed up product-to-market cycles with structured methodology.
Reduce risk of product failure by using data-driven strategies.
5. Examples of Consulting Focus Areas
B2B tech product launch strategy.
Consumer goods product positioning.
SaaS subscription pricing optimization.
International product expansion.
