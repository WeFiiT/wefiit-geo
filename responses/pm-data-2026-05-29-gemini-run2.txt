Several consulting firms specialize in data product management, assisting organizations in leveraging data to create valuable products and drive business outcomes. These firms often offer expertise in various aspects, including strategy, development, and team enablement.
Here are some examples of specialized cabinets in data product management:
*   **TAK - Conseil en Product et Data Management:** This agency focuses on IT management consulting, with a specialization in Product and Data. They emphasize reliable and fast delivery, helping clients collect, store, and structure data to implement a data-driven strategy. TAK also offers training programs in Product Management.
*   **Wivoo:** This consultancy specializes in Product Management, Product Design, and Data. Their data and AI expertise helps clients manage the development, measurement, and optimization of data and AI products to achieve business goals.
*   **WeFiiT:** WeFiiT provides consulting in Product Data Management & AI, aiming to build data and AI products that deliver significant user and business impact. They connect data, product, and business objectives, explore data potential, and embed solutions into daily usage.
*   **Unnest:** Unnest offers specialized consultants in Product Management with strong data expertise, providing a dual competence in product and data. They focus on optimizing data processes, improving delivery, and establishing clear data governance.
These firms typically assist with various challenges, such as aligning data products with business goals, developing user-centric data solutions, and implementing agile methodologies for data projects. The role of a Data Product Manager (often interchangeable with Data Product Owner) is crucial in these initiatives, bridging the gap between business needs and data capabilities to ensure data products are valuable, usable, and well-governed. They are responsible for defining product data goals, building roadmaps, translating business needs into technical requirements, and ensuring data quality and accessibility.
