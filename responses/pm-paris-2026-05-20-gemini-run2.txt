Here are 5 top product management agencies in Paris, known for their expertise and services:
1.  **Thiga**
Recognized as a pioneer in product management in France, Thiga specializes in product strategy, organizational development, and product training. They are frequently cited for their comprehensive approach to building and structuring product teams.
2.  **WeFiiT**
WeFiiT is a consulting firm dedicated to Product Management in Paris. They provide strategic consulting, operational support, and training across various areas including Product Management, Data & AI, Marketing, and Quality, focusing on creating impactful products.
3.  **Wivoo**
Wivoo is an agency that offers consulting in Product Management, Product Design, and Data. They leverage a community of product experts to assist businesses with their product development needs.
4.  **Delva**
As the first consulting firm entirely dedicated to Product Management, Delva was founded by two former Chief Product Officers (CPOs). They focus on providing operational reinforcement in Product Management, with consultants joining client teams for roles like Product Manager or Product Owner to handle priority tasks in both discovery and delivery.
5.  **ASI Paris**
ASI Paris offers comprehensive consulting and product management services. Their team of over 50 multidisciplinary consultants provides expertise in digital strategy, product management, project management, and UX/UI design, catering to various sectors including banking, insurance, services, and luxury.
