1. What is Data Product Management (DPM)?
Data Product Management is a discipline at the intersection of product management, data science, and analytics. It focuses on turning data into valuable products or services, like:
Data platforms
Analytics dashboards
Machine learning models as products
APIs providing data services
A Data Product Manager (DPM) is responsible for:
Defining the strategy for data products
Prioritizing features based on business value
Ensuring data quality, compliance, and accessibility
Coordinating between data engineers, data scientists, and business stakeholders
2. What is a “cabinet spécialisé” in this context?
A cabinet spécialisé is a consulting firm or professional services company that helps organizations:
Define and implement Data Product strategies
Set up data governance and infrastructure
Train or provide Data Product Managers
Optimize data monetization or operational data usage
Think of it like a “product management consultancy” but specifically for data-driven products.
3. Why companies hire them
Companies often hire these cabinets because:
They lack internal expertise in data productization
They want faster, structured adoption of data products
They need guidance on best practices, KPIs, and governance
They want to bridge the gap between data teams and business units
4. Examples of services a specialized cabinet might offer
Assessment of current data assets and identification of data product opportunities
Roadmap creation for building and scaling data products
Product management training for data teams
Governance and compliance frameworks for sensitive data
Implementation support with agile workflows for data products
