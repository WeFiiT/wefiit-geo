Several specialized firms in France offer expertise in data product management, helping companies transform data into valuable products and drive business growth. These cabinets typically provide a range of services from strategic consulting to operational reinforcement and training.
Here are some prominent firms specializing in data product management:
*   **WeFiiT** is a consultancy in Paris that specializes in Product Management, including Product Data & AI. They assist clients with strategic consulting, operational support, and training. WeFiiT focuses on building high-impact products and works with both large enterprises and scale-ups to structure and strengthen their product teams. They emphasize combining a product approach with Data & AI expertise to create data products with measurable ROI.
*   **Yield Advisory** offers experts in Data, AI, and Product Management who integrate with client teams to leverage data for growth and decision-making. They cover the spectrum from strategic audit to industrialization, combining business vision, technical excellence, and product practices to maximize the impact of data projects. Their services include Data Science, Data Analysis, and Data Product Management, where they orchestrate data strategy, prioritize high-value initiatives, and structure roadmaps.
*   **TAK** is a consulting agency specializing in Product and Data Management, focusing on reliable and fast delivery. They position themselves as a community of experts in Product Management oriented towards delivery, aiming to craft tailor-made products. They also offer à la carte training to develop Product Management skills within client teams.
*   **Agence 5 Degrés** helps organizations transform their product data into a strategic management tool, ensuring data serves decision-making. They support clients in implementing data strategies aligned with their product vision and foster informed decision-making. Their experts assist in creating a data culture, transforming data usage, and ensuring the quality and robustness of data solutions.
*   **Elevate** is a data and marketing technologies agency that helps manage data products to ensure each project generates value and meets business expectations. Their services include data strategy, change management, and acculturation/training in data-related fields, including Data Product Owner/Product Manager roles.
*   **Unnest** offers consultants specialized in Product Management with strong data expertise. They possess a dual competence in Product and Data and promote an innovative vision of the Data Product Owner role. They offer flexible intervention methods and focus on optimizing data processes and establishing clear data governance.
*   **AVISIA** positions itself as a large independent cabinet specializing in Data, offering consultants for roles such as Project Manager Data/IA and Product Owner Data/IA. Their Product Owners lead data & AI products from ideation to industrialization, emphasizing agility, prioritization, and business impact.
*   **Gradiant** is a consulting firm specializing in Data, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Data Management, Data Governance, Analytics, and Data-related transformations. They offer expertise in opportunity studies, transformation framing, change management, and operational project management.
*   **Adone Conseil** provides product information consulting, including product data analysis and strategy. They help develop clear roadmaps for managing product information (PIM/PLM/MDM/DAM) aligned with business objectives and digital customer experience. They also assist in selecting suitable technological solutions and managing digital integration projects.
These firms cater to various needs, from defining data product strategies and building data architectures to improving data quality, governance, and operational efficiency through data-driven products.
