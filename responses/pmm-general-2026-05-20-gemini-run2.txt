Several consulting firms and individual consultants specialize in product marketing management, offering expertise to help businesses strategize, execute, and optimize their product launches and market presence. These firms often work with companies to define product positioning, messaging, go-to-market strategies, and overall product lifecycle management.
Here are some notable firms and their areas of focus:
*   **Fluvio** is a product marketing consultancy that integrates senior product marketing consultants into client teams. They assist with scaling strategy, refining execution, and leading critical go-to-market efforts for high-growth B2B SaaS companies, consumer tech brands, and fintech companies. Fluvio's services include expert buyer and customer research, pricing and packaging strategy, and talent placement for product marketing roles.
*   **WeFiiT** offers product marketing consulting to transform products into growth drivers. They help companies with product marketing challenges, including team upskilling, mastering fundamentals like positioning, messaging, and go-to-market, audience targeting through persona studies, and leveraging AI for competitive intelligence.
*   **PJM Consulting** focuses on product management and marketing for software and hardware companies. They provide assistance with strategic and tactical marketing issues, offering advice on software marketing strategy and even interim CMO or VP Marketing services. PJM Consulting specializes in optimizing marketing structure, strategy, tactics, and results for CEOs and senior executives, particularly for startups, turnarounds, and hyper-growth situations.
*   **Cube Management** provides product marketing consulting services, including new product launches, product management, product positioning and messaging, and product life cycle development. They aim to help companies optimize sales performance with well-packaged, positioned, and priced products.
*   **Olivine** offers product marketing consulting services with a focus on aligning and strategizing go-to-market efforts. They provide templates, resources, support, and high-level management for seamless project execution, acting as strategic partners to client teams. Their services encompass positioning and messaging strategy, persona development, GTM strategy, product and feature launches, and hiring support.
*   **Digital Thinkers** is a business consulting firm offering corporate consulting and management advisement, with clients appreciating their seamless integration as an in-house product management team.
*   **McKinsey & Company**, a large global consulting firm, offers management consulting, operational strategy, product and experience design, and risk management. They have a dedicated sales and marketing strategy offering that includes branding, customer insights, pricing, and marketing ROI.
*   **Bain & Company** is another top management consulting firm that advises leaders on strategy, marketing, organization, operations, and IT across various industries.
*   **Individual consultants** such as Harvey, Pablo Asensio (8percent Growth), Elina Bazinas (True North PMM), and Shari (Diamond Advisors) also provide specialized product marketing consulting services. Harvey focuses on strategy planning, messaging, positioning, product innovation, and portfolio optimization. Pablo Asensio assists B2B SaaS companies with growth bottlenecks, go-to-market strategy, and pipeline growth. Elina Bazinas helps scale companies through product launches, new category narratives, and global go-to-market strategies. Shari leads a boutique consultancy to help companies execute product marketing strategies and find PMM talent.
