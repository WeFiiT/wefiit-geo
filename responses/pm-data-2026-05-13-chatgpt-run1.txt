Ah, you’re looking for a “cabinet spécialisé en Data Product Management”, meaning a consultancy or firm that specifically helps organizations manage data as a product. Let’s break this down and give practical guidance.
1. What a “Data Product Management” Cabinet Does
A specialized cabinet (consultancy) focuses on:
Data Strategy & Governance
Define data as a product: ownership, lifecycle, quality metrics.
Help set up data governance frameworks and processes.
Data Product Lifecycle
From discovery → design → development → deployment → maintenance.
Help teams think about data pipelines, APIs, analytics dashboards, or ML datasets as products with users.
Training & Coaching
Upskilling teams on Data Product Management best practices.
Supporting roles like Data Product Managers, Data Engineers, and Analysts.
Organization & Operating Model
Design cross-functional teams (data product managers, engineers, analysts) for better data adoption.
Introduce product-thinking in data teams, not just analytics.
Tools & Platform Recommendations
Advise on data catalogs, pipelines, MLOps, and analytics platforms.
