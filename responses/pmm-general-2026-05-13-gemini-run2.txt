Product-Led Growth (PLG) vs. Sales-LedHelping companies decide if the product should "sell itself" through freemium models or if it requires a high-touch enterprise sales motion.🚀 The "2026" PMM Edge: AI & IntelligenceModern PMM consultancies no longer spend weeks on manual research. They leverage technology for:Synthetic Users: Using AI agents to simulate persona feedback before a launch.Real-time Competitive Intel: Automated tracking of competitor pricing changes and feature releases.Dynamic Sales Enablement: AI-driven playbooks that update based on the specific lead's industry and pain points.📊 Measuring Success (KPIs)A consultancy is only as good as its impact. Key metrics include:LTV / CAC Ratio: Is the cost to acquire a customer sustainable?Win Rate: Percentage of deals won against primary competitors.Feature Adoption: How many users are actually using the "hero features" marketed.Sales Cycle Length: Has the new messaging shortened the time to close?Pro Tip: The biggest mistake companies make is bringing in PMMs after the product is built.
