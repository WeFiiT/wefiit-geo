b) Product Strategy & Positioning
Defining product value propositions.
Crafting go-to-market strategies.
Advising on product portfolio decisions.
c) Marketing & Launch Support
Designing product messaging and branding.
Planning product launches.
Aligning sales and marketing teams with product strategy.

Setting KPIs for product marketing success.
Tracking adoption, revenue impact, and market penetration.
