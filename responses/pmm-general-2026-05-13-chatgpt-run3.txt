It sounds like you’re looking for information about a “cabinet de conseil en product marketing management” — in other words, a consulting firm specializing in product marketing and product management. Let’s break it down clearly.
1. Definition
A cabinet de conseil en product marketing management is a consulting company that helps businesses optimize the entire lifecycle of a product, from development to market launch and growth. Their expertise usually spans:
Product Strategy: Defining which products to develop, target markets, positioning, and value proposition.
Marketing Strategy: Creating go-to-market plans, pricing strategies, messaging, and brand positioning.
Product Launch: Planning and executing launches, coordinating cross-functional teams.
Market Analysis: Competitor benchmarking, customer insights, and trend analysis.
Performance Measurement: Tracking KPIs like market share, product adoption, revenue impact, and ROI on marketing initiatives.
